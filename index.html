REGISTRATIONS...


Register for training by coming directly to our office with the requirements, namely photocopy of ID card, photocopy of diploma, and 1 sheet 3x4 photo, or online (click on the training list menu, the requirements are brought during selection). After that, we will text you when the selection day approaches. Then comes the selection (written test on vocational, and interview). After that, it will be announced who passed the same day, and immediately re-registered to the 3in1 kiosk. After that, join the training until it's finished.
